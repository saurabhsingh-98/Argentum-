# Contributing to Argentum

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Argentum! We welcome community involvement to help build the future of localized networking.

## Our Approach
Argentum is a commercially protected project. This allows us to stay sustainable and continue developing premium features. While the project is not "Open Source" in the traditional sense, it is "Source Available" for contribution.

## How to Contribute
1. **Explore**: Browse the issues or suggest a new feature.
2. **Fork**: Create your own copy of the repository.
3. **Commit**: Work on your changes in a dedicated branch.
4. **Pull Request**: Submit your changes for review.

## Contribution Agreement
By submitting a Pull Request, you agree to the terms of our [LICENSE](LICENSE). Specifically:
- You grant the project owner a non-exclusive, perpetual, and royalty-free license to use and monetize your contributions.
- You confirm that you have the right to submit the code.

## Code of Conduct
Please be respectful and professional in all interactions. We aim to build a supportive community of builders.
